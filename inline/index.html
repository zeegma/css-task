Our Story: A Movement
Born from Love

Sintang Pusa began in 2019 as a Facebook page celebrating the cats of PUP Sta. Mesa. Inspired by the memory of Pogi, a beloved tabby whose tragic loss united students, faculty, and alumni, we've grown into a movement dedicated to protecting and caring for our campus cats through love, awareness, and charitable efforts.

Our Goals: Creating a Better
Future for PUP's Cats

Awareness

Advertise and raise awareness about the cats in PUP.

Feeding

Feed the cats residing within the campus.

Monitoring

Regularly monitor resident cats to ensure their health and safety.

Support

Manage donations and raise funds to sustain the cats' needs and organizational operations.

Volunteers

Gather volunteers to easily mobilize the purpose of the organization

Networking

Reach out to the community to form networks that will benefit the cats.
